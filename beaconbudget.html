Built differently on purpose.

We made the hard choices so your family doesn't have to.

Everything local first

Budgets, transactions, projections, and reports are calculated on your Mac using SwiftData and on-device encryption. No cloud sync. No company database of your net worth.

Credentials stay in Keychain

Optional email scanning uses your mail provider's app password stored only in the macOS Keychain (device-only access). We never see or store it.

Local intelligence only

We are not using AI in Beacon Budget today. If we add intelligent features later, they will use local models that run entirely on your Mac and never leave your device. This gives you strong protection, even if it limits some real-time external capabilities.

Zero-based budgeting

Every dollar gets a job. Income minus allocations equals zero. Giving and stewardship categories are first-class citizens in the budget by design.

Import your way

Drag in statements, point a watch folder at downloads, or let the optional local email scanner pull transactions from bank alerts. You control it.

Built for real families

Cash transactions for tithes and gifts. Annual reviews with a stewardship lens. Privacy Mode for screenshots or support without exposing your real numbers.
