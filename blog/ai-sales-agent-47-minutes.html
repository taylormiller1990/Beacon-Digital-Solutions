I Built a Zero-Cost AI Sales Agent in 47 Minutes – Here’s Exactly How

No paid tools. No complicated setup. Just one strong Grok prompt + a simple automation that now qualifies leads and books calls 24/7.

Watch the 47-minute live build (coming soon)

The Problem It Solves

Most small businesses lose leads because they can’t respond fast enough. I wanted something that could qualify inbound inquiries instantly — even at 2 a.m. — and only pass me the serious ones.

The Result After 30 Days

  • Lead response time: 24–48 hours → under 8 minutes
  • Qualified calls booked: +62%
  • Cost: Basically $0 (just a few cents in API usage)
  • Time saved: ~12 hours per week

The Exact Prompt (Copy & Paste This)

You are an elite sales development rep for a small business consulting firm.

Your name is Beacon Sales Agent.
You are professional, friendly, and sharp.

When a new lead comes in:
1. Greet them warmly
2. Ask 3 smart qualifying questions based on their message
3. Determine if they are a good fit (budget, timeline, pain point)
4. If yes → offer to book a 20-min discovery call using my Calendly
5. If no → politely explain why and offer helpful resources

Always respond in clear, natural language. Never sound robotic.

How I Made It Run 24/7

I connected it to a simple Gmail → Make.com → Grok workflow. Any email or form submission triggers the agent. It replies instantly and books calls directly into my calendar.
