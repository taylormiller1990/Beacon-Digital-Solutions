Privacy Policy

We collect almost nothing because we believe your financial life is none of our business.

Last updated: 2026

Our Core Commitment

Beacon Digital Solutions and Beacon Budget are built so that your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it. This is not a marketing claim — it is an architectural decision we make at every layer.

What Beacon Budget Collects

  • Nothing by default. The app runs entirely locally using SwiftData on your Mac. No accounts are created with us. No telemetry or analytics are sent.
  • Optional email scanning: If you choose to connect your email for transaction import, credentials are stored only in the macOS Keychain (device-only). We never see or transmit them.
  • Optional market data: Ticker symbols only are sent to Yahoo Finance for price quotes. No account data or balances ever leave.
  • Optional AI features (future): When enabled, only anonymized, aggregated summaries are sent. Raw transactions, balances, institution names, and personal details stay on your device.

What We Do Not Do

  • No cloud storage of your financial information
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of any user data
  • No advertising or third-party tracking
  • No building of user profiles for any purpose
  • No requirement to create an account with Beacon Digital Solutions

Subscriptions & the Web

When you subscribe to Premium directly on our website (the path we prefer), we process payment through standard secure processors. We store only the minimal information needed to manage your subscription and provide support. We do not receive or store the detailed financial data inside your Beacon Budget app.

Your Rights

You own your data. You can delete everything from within the app at any time. Because we don't have your data on our servers, there is very little we can "hand over" even if compelled. We design the product this way on purpose.

Why We're Doing This

We aim to treat people's information with the same care we would want for our own. Tools should not require users to give up their privacy or attention in exchange for basic functionality. Protecting time and data is a basic responsibility of good software.

Questions? Contact us at support@beacondigitalsolutionsllc.com. We are happy to explain exactly how anything works.
