Hi, I'm Taylor.

I build tools that protect people's information and time instead of taking them.

I started Beacon Digital Solutions after seeing how easily technology can take more than it gives — collecting data and attention without clear benefit to the user.

We build tools on a clear principle: your data belongs to you. Our products are designed so that we never see your information and never ask for it. All processing that can happen on your device does. This is not something you have to configure — it is how the tools are built.

We offer our tools directly and keep the relationship simple. The goal is to create software that helps you manage responsibilities while protecting the information that is yours.

What we're building

A set of tools designed so that your information never leaves your device. It begins with Beacon Budget, a personal finance application offered with a free version and a premium tier for additional features.

We also provide straightforward resources to help people use these tools effectively.

Based in Jupiter, Florida. Husband, father, and builder focused on tools that serve people rather than the other way around.
